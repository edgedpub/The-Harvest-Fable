Once upon a time in a far country, there lived a rabbit and a badger who lived side by side and each one had a crop field and a house. They got on well together and both enjoyed life in the fields. The rabbit spent most of the time during spring and summer working on his field and during autumn and winter, he had enough to eat and to withstand the cold season. The badger, on the other hand, was lazy and clumsy and his harvest was always meagre and consisted mainly of wheat and peanut plants – his favourite foods.
